## What AlfredScholar does

AlfredScholar is an AI-powered academic research platform that helps researchers, students, and academics manage the research workflow in one workspace. It combines document library management, AI document chat, citation management, manuscript writing, peer review, plagiarism checks, and collaboration.

## Core product areas

- Research Library: Upload, organize, annotate, and search PDF research papers. PDFs are indexed for library-wide search.
- AI Chat: Ask natural language questions about uploaded papers and get answers grounded in source documents with page citations like `[p.12]`.
- Citations: Import BibTeX and RIS, auto-extract references from PDFs, look up DOIs, and export citations in styles such as APA, MLA, Chicago, IEEE, Harvard, and Vancouver.
- Manuscripts: Write academic papers in a rich-text editor with auto-save, citation insertion, tables, images, and submission guideline support.
- Peer Reviews: Share manuscripts with reviewers, collect structured feedback, and manage review cycles.
- Plagiarism Check: Run similarity checks before submission to catch unintentional overlap.
- Collaboration: Use workspaces, invite colleagues or reviewers, and coordinate shared research work.

## PhD student use cases

- Literature reviews: Upload papers, compare methodologies, summarize findings, and identify gaps with cited answers.
- Thesis writing: Write drafts with auto-save, word count tracking, citation support, and submission guideline validation.
- Citation management: Import existing BibTeX or RIS libraries, extract references from PDFs, and format citations.
- Pre-submission checks: Run plagiarism checks before committee or journal submission.
- Advisor collaboration: Share workspaces and manuscripts with advisors, colleagues, or reviewers.
- Reading and annotating: Read papers in the browser, highlight passages, add notes, and review highlights.

## AI chat details

- Users upload PDFs, ask questions in plain English, and get cited answers.
- Alfred uses semantic and full-text hybrid search across uploaded documents.
- Every claim should be traceable to source material with inline page citations.
- Cross-document answers can compare methodologies, findings, theoretical frameworks, limitations, and other themes across a document library.
- Conversation memory supports follow-up questions within a chat.

## Privacy and data positioning

- User research is scoped to the user's workspace.
- Documents are private unless explicitly shared with invited collaborators.
- AlfredScholar says uploaded documents are not used to train AI models.
- The business model is subscription based, not selling user data or mining research.
- Users can export citations, with broader export support described as coming or planned in public copy.

## Pricing observed publicly

Recheck before publishing, because pricing can change.

- Free: ₹0 per month, free forever, no card needed. Includes 10 documents, 100 AI messages per month, Standard AI model, unlimited citations, unlimited manuscripts, 1 plagiarism check per month, BibTeX and RIS import, 1 peer reviewer, colleague invites, and Zotero and Mendeley sync.
- Pro: Listed as ₹499 per month or ₹4,999 per year, with ₹417 per month billed annually. Includes unlimited documents, unlimited AI messages, Advanced AI model, unlimited citations and manuscripts, 5 plagiarism checks per month, all citation styles, 3 peer reviewers, Zotero and Mendeley sync, and email support.
- Lab: Listed as ₹2,499 per month or ₹24,999 per year. Flat rate for up to 10 seats. Includes everything in Pro, 1 owner plus 9 colleagues, unlimited peer reviewers, unlimited plagiarism checks, Frontier AI model, shared workspaces, role-based access control, supervisor review workflow, and WhatsApp priority support.
